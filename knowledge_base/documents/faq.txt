Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You will receive an email with a reset
   link that is valid for 1 hour.

Q: Can I upgrade my plan at any time?
A: Yes. Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. The price difference is prorated for
   the remainder of your current billing cycle.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, all new accounts receive a 14-day free trial of the Business plan with full
   access to all features. No credit card is required to start.

Q: How do I export my data?
A: Navigate to Settings > Data Management > Export. You can export your data in CSV,
   JSON, or PDF format. Exports are available for 7 days after generation.

Q: Does Acme Corp offer an API?
A: Yes. The Acme API is available on the Business and Enterprise plans. Documentation
   is available at https://docs.acmecorp.example.com/api. Rate limits depend on your
   plan tier.

Q: What integrations are supported?
A: Acme products integrate with Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Jira, GitHub,
   and Zapier. Enterprise customers can request custom integrations.
