# Return & Refund Policy

## Eligibility
- Subscription cancellations can be requested at any time through the account dashboard.
- Refunds are available within 14 days of the initial purchase or renewal.
- Partial-month refunds are not provided; the subscription remains active until the end of the billing period.

## How to Request a Refund
1. Log in to your Acme Corp account.
2. Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Subscription**.
3. Click **Request Refund** and fill out the short form.
4. A confirmation email will be sent within 24 hours.

## Processing Time
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval.
- The refund will appear on the original payment method.

## Exceptions
- Annual subscriptions purchased more than 14 days ago are not eligible for a refund
  but can be cancelled to prevent future renewal.
- Promotional or discounted subscriptions are non-refundable.
